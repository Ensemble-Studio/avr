The

PROCESS

 From intake to sourcing the right gem, let us take you trough the steps one by one below.

/01

consult

Each piece of jewellery by Alexandra is uniquely designed for her clients.

/02

Sourcing

All her materials are hand sourced and quality checked by Alexandra and her team of experts in gemmology, gold smithing and stone setting.

/03

Design

Each design starts with you and Alexandra about your wishes and requirements. She then takes the blueprint of your piece to the studio to discuss the drawing with her artisans. When all the stones and metals are sourced and the design is finalised, the design is giving to the studio to be created.

/04

Atelier

The craftsmen of the studio will make each individual piece by hand, taking several weeks to perfect it. After thorough quality control, by Alexandras team, the piece will be handed over to the client.

/05

Delivery

When the final piece is ready for delivery when the payment is complete, the piece will travel by international post or with person.
