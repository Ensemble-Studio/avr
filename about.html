Alexandra van Rijckevorsel is a fine jewellery designed, based in both Amsterdam and Jaipur, the gems and jewellery capital of India.

Her knowledge and expertise come from her lifelong education about jewellery through her family’s business, whilst having spent countless hours amongst the best artisans, goldsmiths and stone cutters in Jaipur. All jewellery made by Alexandra are one of a kind, and tailor made for you.
