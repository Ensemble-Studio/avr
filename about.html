Alexandra van Rijckevorsel is a fine jewellery designer, based in both Amsterdam and Jaipur, the gems and jewellery capital of India.

Gifted to seal special occasions - a jewel always tells a story. words by alexandra
Her knowledge and expertise come from her lifelong education about jewellery through her family’s business, whilst having spent countless hours amongst the best artisans, goldsmiths and stone cutters in Jaipur.

All jewellery made by Alexandra are one of a kind, and tailor made for you.

Do you have a special occassion planned? Plan an intake with Alexandra to discuss your custom made piece.
